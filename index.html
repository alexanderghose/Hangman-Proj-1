Let's Play Hangman!

Rules of the Game:

Hey There!
Start the game by picking the category you want to play. Once the button is clicked, a word will be assigned to you and then you can start guessing!

Some things to keep in mind: if you guess a letter and the next letter you pick replaces the previous one, you're guessing the wrong word. Try another letter and keep guessing until all letters follow each other consecutively!
Good luck!!
